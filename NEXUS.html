Why It Matters

Built for distance, not for smartphones

Al Qua'a sits across a wide desert area. The average informal emergency response time in dispersed rural UAE communities is 18–35 minutes — the window where outcomes turn fatal.

📱

No app, no smartphone needed

Built entirely on WhatsApp messaging — works for elderly residents and basic-phone users who would never download a dedicated app.

🗣️

Arabic & English voice

The AI voice agent speaks naturally in either language, matching whichever the clinic staff respond in.

🌐

Zero-cost, real infrastructure

Runs entirely on free tiers — Make.com, Vapi credits, Twilio sandbox — deployable tomorrow with one laptop and one SIM card.
