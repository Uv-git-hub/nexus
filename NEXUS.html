Why It Matters

Built for distance, not for smartphones

📱

No app needed

WhatsApp on any basic phone. The form on this page. A camera detecting a fall. Three ways to trigger NEXUS — none require a smartphone or internet on the resident's side.

🗣️

Arabic-first voice

SAWT opens every call in Arabic: "مرحباً، أنا NEXUS". Switches to English if clinic staff respond in English. Bilingual, natural, respectful.

🛡️

SENTINEL security

Offline cybersecurity agent monitors for data attacks and spoofed alerts. MOSCA score 0-100 tells you exactly how secure patient data is at any moment.
